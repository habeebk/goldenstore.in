Who we are

Golden Paints is the re-imagined decades old Golden Store business based in Malabar area many many decades ago. We believe in providing best quality products at affordable price

We are the authorised dealer for many high quality paint brands based in India and outside. Since we source directly from paint manufactures without middlemen, our customers gets the best price

Heavy rain in Kerala causes paints to get wash off or fade quickly by end of each season, visit us to know how can we help us to solve this problem with long lasting paints that overcomes seasonal weather.
